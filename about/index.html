Our three-phase roadmap

From deployments to proprietary IP.

PHASE 1 — MARKET ESTABLISHMENT

Prove it in the field.

Fleet platform deployments across school, corporate, and logistics segments in India. Parallel pilot engineering contracts with OEM and Tier-1 clients. Establishing delivery credibility and production-proven references.

PHASE 2 — OFFSHORE ENGINEERING SCALE

Scale the pods.

Dedicated engineering pod expansion for global OEM and Tier-1 programs. Multi-client delivery operations. Deepening connected vehicle capabilities across OTA, remote feature, and test automation domains.

PHASE 3 — PROPRIETARY CONNECTED VEHICLE IP

Convert learnings into products.

OTA testing SDKs, HIL automation frameworks, connected vehicle simulation tooling—and eventually proprietary platform IP with licensing potential.
